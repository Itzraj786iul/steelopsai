# Industrial Operating Rules — Mined from JSPL Historical Data

**Source:** 12,758 heats (`final_model_dataset.csv`)  
**Methods:** Correlation, quartile analysis, decision tree (max_depth=3), partial dependence patterns

---

## Confirmed Rules

1. **HM↑ ↔ DRI trade-off** — Operators balance liquid vs solid feed; HM and DRI show coupled adjustments in optimizer recommendations and plant practice.

2. **HM↑ → Oxygen↓** — Top HM quartile (>61.7 t) averages OXY 3,580 Nm³ vs 3,720 Nm³ for bottom quartile (<55 t).

3. **Bucket↑ → Energy↑** — Heats with scrap bucket >5 t average POWER 34,809 kWh vs 32,917 kWh without bucket charge.

4. **Carbon + Oxygen coupling** — High CPC (>P75) and high OXY (>P75) co-occur in ~18% of heats; these heats average TTT 62.3 min vs 54.1 min for low-low combinations.

5. **High Lime → Longer TTT** — P75 lime heats average TTT 57.9 min vs 50.9 min for P25.

6. **HM quartile vs mean TTT** — Q1: 56.0 min, Q2: 54.9 min, Q3: 56.4 min, Q4: 59.0 min (non-monotonic — delay outliers inflate upper quartile).
