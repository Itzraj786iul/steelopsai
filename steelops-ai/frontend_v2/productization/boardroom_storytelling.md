# Boardroom Storytelling Guide

## Opening line (CEO / Chairman)

> "SteelOps AI saved **₹{savings}** today — that's **{minutes}** minutes back on the floor and **{green}%** GREEN heat efficiency."

## Three questions every board member asks

### 1. How much money did AI save today?
**Panel:** Financial story strip → Story panel "AI saved"  
**Drill:** Savings waterfall, cost breakdown

### 2. Which furnace is costing money?
**Panel:** Plant overview map  
**Look for:** Warning/critical status, low health bar, HIGH risk label

### 3. What is tomorrow's production risk?
**Panel:** Executive narrative + root cause Pareto  
**Look for:** Pending approvals, top causes (High DRI, Late Oxygen)

## Secondary questions

| Question | Where to look |
|----------|----------------|
| Are operators trusting AI? | AI adoption %, operator leaderboard |
| What prevents world-class performance? | Root cause Pareto, shift comparison |
| Is the investment paying off? | AI ROI dashboard |

## Presentation flow (10 min)

1. Financial strip (30 sec)
2. Story panels (1 min)
3. Narrative bullets (2 min)
4. Plant map — focus weakest furnace (2 min)
5. ROI dashboard (2 min)
6. Q&A — performance trends, export snapshot

## Language for the board

- Say **"heat time"** not AT
- Say **"how certain is AI"** not confidence tier
- Say **"money recovered"** not business value INR

## Export

Use **Board presentation** → fullscreen → **Executive PDF** (print) or **JSON snapshot** for finance team.
